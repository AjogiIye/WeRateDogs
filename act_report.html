Project: WeRateDog Udacity¶

1. Introduction¶

WeRateDogs is a twitter handle that rates pictures of dogs that are posted on twitter by different dog owners and people.Data was gathered from csv and tsv files and via twitter APIs to analyze the tweets as part of the aim of this project.A total of 2356 tweets was properly cleaned, investigated, insights were generated and effectively communicated as seen below

first insight¶

WeRateDogs has various rating schemes which is dependent on the investigation been carried out. However, 26% of people did not post dog pictures based on the image prediction.This could stem from the fact that entitle people hardly follow instructions since to them it is 'unfair' as stated by Zitek & Jordan(2017).It is then pertinent to deduce that about 26% of WeRateDogs rendezvous are entitle people.
